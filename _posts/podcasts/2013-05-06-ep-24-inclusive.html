Better late than never?  The overdue 24th episode of the IGDATC podcast has been nursed back to health and released into the wild.  In this episode, hosts Ryan, Tori and Jacque discuss various gaming and game development news with special guests Nate Yourchuck, Martin Grider and Scott Lembcke.  GDC happened, and since Tori and Ryan were unable to attend, Jacque and special quests were.We invite your feedback and comments. Thank you for listening.Show Notes

  • Game Audio, teaching and an app called Audiobus.
  • Dead Space's co-op experience unique to each player.
  • Ryan is a mentor at Coder Dojo Twin Cities.  Also at Mini-bar
  • Jacque hates Tauren Shamens
  • Martin and Scott presented at Mobil March.
  • The IGDA party fiasco at GDC.  The two articles you should read are IGDA's official response and YetiZen's CEO Sana Choudary's response.  Jacque's blog is also a good review of her opinion.
  • Other cool stuff at GDC.  Violence, Game Design Challenge, Experimental Gameplay, more!
  • GDC vault, Fair Use Rant

Special Guests

  • Nate Yourchuck is a freelancer at his company Nimble Gorilla.
  • Martin Grider is president and owner of Abstract Puzzle, an indie game developer
  • Scott Lembcke is co-owner of Howling Moon Software and also the creator of the Chipmunk Physics engine.
